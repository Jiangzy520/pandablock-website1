Perpetual DEX

Haven 1

In this case study, we dive into the creation of a Perpetual Decentralized Exchange (DEX) for Haven1, a blockchain focused on safe and secure Web3 experiences. Our team adapted GMX’s trading architecture to fit Haven1’s unique environment, overcoming hurdles like limited oracle support, network restrictions, and custom deployment needs. The end result? A robust DEX featuring real-time pricing with Redstone oracles, dynamic collateral and risk management, and a user-friendly referrals system. This custom solution empowers Haven1 to offer a reliable, flexible trading experience designed to meet the demands of today’s Web3 users.
